About Me

Currently I am a 4th-year Ph.D candidate with the College of Computing and Data Science(CCDS), Nanyang Technological University(NTU), under the supervision of Prof Yu Han.

Research Interests

My research primarily focuses on Federated Learning (FL), with a particular emphasis on federated networks and the management of FL communities. To advance research in Federated Learning (FL), I developed a simulation platform that offers a user-friendly interface and supports concurrent FL task processing.
